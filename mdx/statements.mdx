My current appointment is as a Teaching Professor, with expectations that I spend 60% of time on teaching, 20% on service, and 20% on scholarly activities.

## Current Statements

* **[Teaching Statement](/teaching)**: I have taught [CS1 at Illinois](https://www.cs124.org/) since I arrived in Fall 2017, and implemented a wide variety of changes and improvements that have supported increased student success in my course and a doubling in enrollment.

* **[Service Statement](/service)**: I have led efforts to improve our undergraduate curriculum and the teaching faculty hiring process. I have also made my CS1 materials publicly available at [`learncs.online`](https://learncs.online).

* **[Statement on Scholarly Activities](/scholarship)**: I have designed, created, deployed, maintained, and evaluated multiple novel systems supporting my course.

My [CV](/CV) provides another overview of my teaching and other activities, and the [projects page](/projects) on this site provides an overview of some of my more significant instructional research and development efforts. I have also published a series of [online essays](/essays) covering various aspects of how I teach my courses and engaging in advocacy on behalf of teaching faculty.
