Why Traditional Assignments Broke

The old anti-cheating approach:

  • Rotate assignments every semester
  • Students can't find old solutions online
  • No tool existed to translate spec → code
We've built that tool. It's called a coding agent.

If the agent can do the entire assignment...
...the student is doing little to nothing.

The Impossible Tension

Accurate specification

AI can do it all

Vague specification

Student frustration

This is almost impossible to resolve with traditional assignments.

Classical Programming Remains

CBTF quizzes: 80% of grade

Why keep it?

  • Clear thinking — decompose problems precisely
  • Accurate expression — say exactly what you mean
  • Reading comprehension — understand complex systems

Like a crossword puzzle for the mind — mental training that transfers.

The New Reality

  • Students do: Idea → Specification (uniquely human)
  • AI does: Specification → Code (what it excels at)
  • Classical programming: Mental training (80% via CBTF)

We need to teach students to work WITH AI, not against it.
